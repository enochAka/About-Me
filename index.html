Hello, my name is Enoch Akande, and I’m proud to call Ireland my home. I was born in Dundalk, County Louth, and I now live in Coolaney, County Sligo, a place that has shaped a lot of who I am today. I currently attend Coola Post Primary School in Riverstown, where I’ve discovered one of my biggest passions in life: coding.

From when I was young, I’ve always been fascinated by how technology works — how apps are built, how websites come to life, and how a few lines of code can turn into something powerful and useful. Over the years, that curiosity has grown into a real ambition. My dream is to become a world‑famous professional programmer, someone whose ideas and creations make a real difference in the world. I want to build tools, apps, and technologies that help people, solve problems, and inspire others to create as well.

I spend a lot of my free time learning new programming languages, experimenting with projects, and exploring different areas of tech. Whether it’s web development, game design, or software engineering, I love challenging myself to understand more and improve my skills. Every project I work on teaches me something new, and every mistake pushes me to get better.

This website is a place where I can share my journey — my projects, my ideas, and the things I’m learning along the way. It’s a space that represents who I am now and who I hope to become in the future. I’m excited to keep growing, keep building, and keep pushing toward my goals.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed creating it.
